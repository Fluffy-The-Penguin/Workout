65kg Lean Bulk Complete Guide

Goal: 2700–2800 kcal | 115–130g Protein | 0.5–1kg Gain per Month

🔥 Daily Targets

  • Maintenance Calories: ~2300 kcal
  • Lean Bulk Target: 2700–2800 kcal/day
  • Protein: 1.6–2.2g per kg bodyweight
  • For 65kg → 105–130g protein daily
  • Water Intake: 3+ Liters Daily

🥣 High Calorie Breakfast

Ingredients:

  • 100g Muesli (22g protein per 100g)
  • 500ml Full Cream Milk
  • 2 tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 1 tbsp Honey

Macros:

  • Calories: 950–1100 kcal
  • Protein: 40–45g
  • Carbs: High (muscle fuel)
  • Healthy Fats: Moderate
Tip: Add 1 banana for extra 100 kcal if weight gain stalls.

🥚 Egg Strategy

  • 4–6 Whole Eggs Daily
  • Optional: 2–3 Extra Egg Whites
  • Cook as Omelet / Boiled / Scrambled

Nutrition (6 Whole Eggs):

  • ~420 kcal
  • ~36g Protein
  • Healthy fats for hormone support

🍛 Lunch & Dinner Structure

  • 2–3 Roti OR 1.5 Cups Rice
  • 150–200g Paneer OR 3 Eggs
  • 1 Bowl Dal (Protein + Fiber)
  • Vegetables for micronutrients
Each main meal should give 30–40g protein.

🏋️ 4-Day Training Split

Monday – Chest + Triceps

  • Bench Press – 4×8–10
  • Incline DB Press – 3×10
  • Tricep Pushdown – 3×12

Tuesday – Back + Biceps

  • Lat Pulldown – 4×10
  • Seated Row – 3×10
  • Barbell Curl – 3×12

Thursday – Legs

  • Squats – 4×8
  • Leg Press – 3×10
  • Leg Curls – 3×12

Saturday – Shoulders + Abs

  • Overhead Press – 4×8
  • Lateral Raises – 3×15
  • Plank – 3 Sets
Progressive Overload: Increase weight once 12 reps feel easy.

💊 Creatine Guide

  • Type: Creatine Monohydrate
  • Dosage: 3–5g Daily
  • No Loading Required
  • Take After Evening Workout
  • Take Even on Rest Days

Expected Results:

  • Week 1–2: Fuller muscles
  • Week 3–4: Noticeable strength increase
  • Long-term: Better performance & growth

📈 Progress Tracking

  • Weigh yourself once weekly (same time)
  • If no gain in 2 weeks → add 100–150 kcal
  • Aim for 0.5kg/month lean gain
  • Track gym strength increases

⚠️ Important Rules

  • Sleep 7–8 Hours
  • Train 4–5 Days Weekly
  • Focus on Form First Month
  • Consistency > Motivation
  • Food Builds Muscle, Not Supplements Alone
